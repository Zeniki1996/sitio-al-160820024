History

Family Commitment to the Best Export Fruit Pitakawsay is a family business, established in 2016. The fruit from Pitakawsay is characterized by the conscientious and loving work of everyone involved in the company. From applying fundamental values such as hard work, respect, and family, to innovating to obtain a quality product that meets all food safety standards.

Where Flavor and Life Meet

The name Pitakawsay is not a random choice; it is a profound representation of our mission. 'Pita' honors the pitahaya, the exotic fruit we export, while 'Kawsay,' a Quechua word, means 'life'. Together, these elements capture our essence: nourishing lives with the freshness and vitality of each pitahaya we bring to the world.

Commitment to Human Talent

We are committed to our collaborators through continuous training, providing them with a dignified and respectful work environment. These factors are essential to ensure the production of high-quality fruit and a responsible operation.

Values

Our company is characterized by its responsibility, the creation of an optimal work environment, the commitment to obtaining a high-quality product, adaptability, dedication to constant training in new technologies, and diligence in all our activities.

Mission

Our mission is to offer the best pitahaya, meeting all food safety requirements and standards, while placing special emphasis on training our employees and monitoring customer satisfaction to ensure a high-quality product.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading exporter of Ecuadorian pitahaya, standing out for the excellence of our product and expanding our market to different countries.
