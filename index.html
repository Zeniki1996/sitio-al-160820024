Welcome to
Pitakawsay

Since 2016, at Pitakawsay we specialize in cultivating and offering the highest quality pitahaya, complying with strict national and international standards. Located in the province of Manabí, Ecuador, we take pride in every fruit we produce, ensuring that each pitahaya that reaches your table is nutritious and delicious.

Our
Dragon Fruit

The Red Pitahaya with White Pulp (Hylocereus undatus), also known as "Dragon Fruit," is an exotic fruit with a striking appearance and a distinctive sweet-and-sour flavor. Its bright skin and juicy pulp provide a refreshing and revitalizing experience, making it a healthy option to enjoy.

Nutritional
Benefits

Dragon Fruit is not only visually appealing, but also offers multiple health benefits. It is an excellent choice for those who want to maintain a healthy and balanced diet.

Our
Certifications

"Buenas Prácticas Agropecuarias"
(BPA)

It is an official recognition that guarantees an agricultural producer meets a series of quality and food safety standards.

Global
G.A.P.

It is a global standard for Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI).
