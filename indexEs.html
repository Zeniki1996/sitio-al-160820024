Growing the Best Pitahaya for You

At Pitakawsay, we specialize in cultivating and providing the highest quality pitahaya from the province of Manabí, Ecuador. As dedicated producers, we take pride in every fruit we grow, ensuring that each pitahaya that reaches your table is fresh, nutritious, and delicious. We work tirelessly with our farmers in Manabí to guarantee that each pitahaya is grown under the highest quality standards, bringing you the best of nature with every bite.

Our Fruit

The Red Pitahaya with White Pulp (Hylocereus undatus) is an exotic gem from nature, known for its dazzling appearance and unparalleled taste. Its vibrant skin and juicy white pulp create a unique sensory experience. It's not only a delight for the palate but also a source of health.

Health Benefits

Strengthens the immune system

Thanks to its vitamin C and antioxidants, pitahaya helps to strengthen the body's defenses.

Improves digestive health

The fiber in pitahaya promotes the proper functioning of the digestive system and prevents constipation.

Promotes skin health

The antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties of pitahaya can help improve the appearance of the skin and slow aging.

Helps control blood sugar

The fiber in pitahaya helps to regulate blood sugar levels, making it a suitable choice for people with diabetes or prediabetes..

Our Certifications

Our commitment as a company is to offer our customers the highest quality fruit, using organic products and complying with all relevant guidelines related to food safety standards.

Good Agricultural Practices

These are a set of principles, standards, and technical recommendations applicable to the production, processing, and transportation of food, aimed at protecting human health, the environment, and improving the conditions of workers and their families.

Global GAP

Supports the continuous improvement of agricultural systems with a holistic approach to farm assurance including food safety, environment and biodiversity, worker welfare, traceability, and animal welfare.

Fruit fly Free Crop

Cultivation Certification granted to production sites that comply with rigorous monitoring through official traps and proper integrated management that guarantees the absence of the Fruit Fly
