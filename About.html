ILLUMINATING GROWTH:

Our pitahaya blooms at night

Lights are placed in the pitahaya plantations at night as a strategy to stimulate their flowering. In addition to their technical function, the lights create a spectacular landscape, illuminating the fields in the darkness of the night.

OUR NAME:

Where flavor and life meet

The name Pitakawsay is not a random choice; it is a profound representation of our mission. 'Pita' refers to the pitahaya, the exotic fruit we export, while 'Kawsay', a Quechua word, means 'life'. Together, these elements capture our essence: nourishing lives with the freshness and vitality of each pitahaya we bring to the world.

Commitment to Human Talent

We commit to our employees through continuous training, providing them with a dignified and respectful work environment.
