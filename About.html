History

Pitakawsay is a family-owned company, founded in 2016 with the purpose of producing export-quality fruit. The fruit of Pitakawsay is characterized by the conscientious and loving work of all those who make up the company. From the application of fundamental values such as work, respect, and family, to innovating in technology to obtain a quality product that meets all food safety standards. Commitment to Human Talent We are committed to our collaborators through continuous training, providing them with a dignified and respectful work environment. These factors are essential to ensure the production of high-quality fruit and a responsible operation.

Mission

We believe in the potential and quality of the Ecuadorian fruit, as well as in our commitment as a company to grow in the international market. Our mission is to be able to offer the best dragon fruit, complying with all food safety requirements and standards, as well as placing special emphasis on training our employees, and keeping track on our customers and their levels of satisfaction with our services, to achieve a high quality product.

Vision

Our vision is to become the main exporter of Ecuadorian Dragon fruit, with our top quality product and to expand our market to different countries.
